Who is Dottie Mae?

Dottie Mae also known as Dottie Girl is our fur baby. She came to live with our family when she was three months old and has captured our hearts and minds ever since. We went from a family that did not allow dogs inside the house to a family that loves to have this precious living being inside the house.

On any given day, she can be seen peeking out the window at passer bys or barking at the mail man in an effort to protect from intruders. Small in size, but ferocious and protective in heart.

About Yorkies: Small Dog, Big Personality!

Most people have heard of Yorkie dogs but there is also a cousin of the breed known as the Silky Yorkie. The distinctive characteristics of a Silky are as follows: 6-7 inches tall but longer than the standard Yorkie, distinctive slate blue/ pigeon blue/ silver blue or silver coats with tan markings, almond shaped eyes with distinctive black outlines, high tails and slightly more protective of their owners.

Silky Yorkies hail from Australia where it is known as the Australian terrier. In contrast to its English work-dog cousin the Yorkie, the Silky is generally known as a companion dog but is still a hard working little dog.
